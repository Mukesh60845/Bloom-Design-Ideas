How to make micro-interactions that delight

COVER IMAGE / ILLUSTRATION

Micro-interactions are the small details that make your product feel alive. They are brief, delightful, and they convey clarity.

Start with intention

Every animation or little feedback should have a purpose — to guide, to reassure or to surprise pleasantly. Keep them short (200–500ms) and consistent.

Tip: Use easing curves that match physical sense — try cubic-bezier(.2,.9,.3,1) for friendly finishes.

Accessibility & performance

Always provide a reduced-motion alternative and ensure interactions don't cause layout shifts. Prioritize readability over fancy effects.
